# Cookie Policy - VentureLens AI

VentureLens AI uses cookies to optimize user experience and session management.

---

## 1. What are Cookies?
Cookies are small text files stored on your device when you access websites.

## 2. Cookies We Use
- **Strictly Necessary Cookies**: Session identification tokens for Clerk Authentication.
- **Analytical/Performance Cookies**: OpenTelemetry tracking cookies to monitor API request latency and errors.

## 3. Managing Cookies
You can disable cookies in your browser settings, but doing so will prevent you from signing in and using the dashboard.
